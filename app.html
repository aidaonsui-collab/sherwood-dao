How the APY works

APY is emissions, not profit: rebases dilute every unstaked holder while your staked share of supply stays roughly constant. The real return is the treasury compounding underneath — staking simply keeps your fraction of the forest whole.

SherwoodDAO is a treasury-backed reserve currency. Every WOOD is minted against a treasury of real-world assets — tokenized T-bills, USDG and tokenized equities — and every figure on this page is computed from live balances, not promises.

Price is tethered to backing in both directions from launch: bonds fill the treasury, the treasury defends the band, and the band disciplines the premium. A floor you can audit — not one you have to trust.
